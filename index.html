Matej Latin
UX Designer. Minimalist.

My story began in a small seaside town of Koper in Slovenia. Aged 13, I joined an after–school class where I handcrafted my first website and it changed my life. It made me who I am today—it made me a designer. My passion for simple, clean & usable design took me to a journey through Germany, Luxembourg and dropped me off in London, UK—where I currently work as a product designer at DueDil.

I spend my free time working on personal side projects and create things like Guthenberg—Web Type Starter Kit and Better Web Type.
